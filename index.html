The Housing Challenge

New York City's housing crisis hits returning citizens hardest. With average rents exceeding $3,000 and limited affordable options, finding stable housing becomes nearly impossible. Many face homelessness or return to unstable situations, increasing recidivism rates.

Rebase's directory simplifies the process, connecting individuals with local housing resources, case management, and support services to break this cycle.

The Rebase Project

Founded in 2025 in partnership with New York re-entry organizations, Rebase emerged from the recognition that stable housing is the foundation of successful re-entry, but is so hard to find in the city. We've built the most comprehensive housing directory in NYC, connecting thousands of people with the resources they need to rebuild their lives.
