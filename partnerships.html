Our Partnerships

Working together with organizations across New York to expand housing access and support services for ex-incarcerated individuals.

I

Housing Providers

Halfway houses, transitional housing, and permanent supportive housing organizations that provide direct services to returning citizens.

Service Organizations

Nonprofits and agencies that provide case management, employment support, mental health services, and substance abuse treatment.

Government Agencies

City and state agencies that fund housing programs and provide oversight for re-entry services.
